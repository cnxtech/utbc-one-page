Exclusive Sponsor for Badminton Equipment & Services

Membership

UTBC offers a number of benefits and services for our members for price of $30 per school year.

  • Court Hours & Coaching

    Come and meet other badminton enthusiasts, and play on our weekly court hours dedicated to our members. Improve your skills with our volunteer coaches.

  • Tournaments

    Take your skills to test by competing with other students in a variety of tournament styles to win great badminton merchandise and prizes.

  • String Service

    Bring in your rackets to get them restringed with our sponsors at a reasonable price and collect them back at our booth.

  • Discounted Merchandise

    Buy badminton rackets, bags, shuttles and other badminton merchandise from our sponsors at discounted prices.
